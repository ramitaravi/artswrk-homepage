## Idea 1: Bold Editorial — "Arts Magazine"

**Design Movement:** Bold editorial / contemporary arts magazine (think Dazed & Confused meets a modern SaaS landing page)

**Core Principles:**
- High-contrast black and white base with vivid color pops from the brand palette (coral/orange gradient)
- Typography does the heavy lifting — oversized display text, tight tracking, mixed weights
- Photography is the hero — full-bleed artist images with minimal UI chrome
- Asymmetric layouts that feel curated, not templated

**Color Philosophy:** White canvas with near-black (#0a0a0a) text. The orange-to-coral gradient (#F97316 → #E91E8C) is reserved exclusively for CTAs, logo, and accent labels — making them pop with maximum contrast. No purple, no blue.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric split columns. Hero is left-heavy text + right-side dancer image. Sections alternate text-left/image-right and image-left/text-right. The artist strip is a full-width horizontal scroll ticker.

**Signature Elements:**
- Pill-shaped toggle for "for hirers / for artists" in orange gradient
- Eyebrow labels in small orange caps (e.g., "FOR HIRERS", "HOW IT WORKS")
- Dark app mockup cards with rounded corners and subtle shadow

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states reveal color. Cards lift slightly on hover. The image ticker scrolls infinitely and pauses on hover.

**Animation:** Entrance animations are subtle — text fades up on scroll. The artist strip uses CSS infinite scroll animation. CTA button has a warm glow pulse.

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Syne" (bold, 700/800) for headlines — geometric, modern, slightly quirky
- Body: "DM Sans" (400/500) — clean, readable, neutral
- Labels: "DM Sans" 500, uppercase, small tracking

## Idea 2: Warm Brutalism — "Studio Wall"

**Design Movement:** Warm brutalism / zine aesthetic — raw but intentional, like a well-designed flyer on a dance studio bulletin board

**Core Principles:**
- Thick borders, offset shadows (no blur — hard shadows only)
- Warm off-white background (#FAF7F2) instead of pure white
- Text is unapologetically large and stacked
- Grid breaks intentionally — elements overlap slightly

**Color Philosophy:** Warm cream base. The brand orange/coral is used liberally for borders, backgrounds of accent cards, and button fills. Black is used for borders and hard shadows. No gradients except on the logo.

**Layout Paradigm:** Stacked full-width sections with thick top borders. Cards use hard drop shadows (4px offset, no blur). The hero is a single massive headline with a small CTA below — no split layout.

**Signature Elements:**
- Hard-shadow cards with 3px black borders
- Sticker-style eyebrow labels (orange background, black text, slight rotation)
- Monospaced step numbers for How It Works

**Interaction Philosophy:** Buttons press down on click (translateY). Cards shift their shadow on hover to simulate picking up.

**Animation:** Minimal — only the ticker scrolls. No scroll animations. Fast and snappy.

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Space Grotesk" (700) — geometric, slightly quirky, great for large sizes
- Body: "Space Grotesk" (400) — consistent system
- Labels: "Space Mono" (400) — monospaced, adds rawness

## Idea 3: Refined Modern — "Premium Platform" ✅ CHOSEN

**Design Movement:** Refined modern SaaS / premium marketplace — clean, confident, and energetic without being cold

**Core Principles:**
- White background with generous whitespace — the content breathes
- The orange-coral gradient is the brand's heartbeat — used for the logo, CTAs, and accent labels
- Typography is bold and confident for headlines, clean and readable for body
- Photography is vibrant and full of personality — the artists ARE the product

**Color Philosophy:** Pure white (#FFFFFF) base. Near-black (#111111) for all text. The gradient from coral (#F97316) to hot pink (#E91E8C) is used for: logo "ARTS" text, CTA buttons, eyebrow labels, and active toggle states. This creates a warm, energetic brand identity that feels human and creative.

**Layout Paradigm:** Centered hero with full-width sections below. The hero is centered and clean. Sections use a 2-column split (text + visual mockup). The artist strip is a full-width infinite scroll ticker. Business cards are a 3-column grid with image backgrounds.

**Signature Elements:**
- Gradient logo text "ARTS" + bold black "WRK" pill badge
- Pill-shaped "for hirers / for artists" toggle with gradient active state
- App UI mockup screenshots in dark-bordered rounded frames

**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth hover transitions (150ms ease). FAQ accordion with smooth expand/collapse. Image cards darken slightly on hover.

**Animation:** Fade-up on scroll for section content. Infinite CSS ticker for artist strip and logo strip. Smooth accordion for FAQ.

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Syne" (700/800) — bold, modern, slightly editorial
- Body: "DM Sans" (400/500) — clean, highly readable

## Chosen Design: Idea 3 — Refined Modern "Premium Platform"

Syne for display headlines, DM Sans for body. White background, near-black text, orange-coral gradient as the brand accent. Clean centered hero, 2-column feature sections, infinite ticker strips, 3-column business cards, and a clean FAQ accordion.
